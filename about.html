About Doward - Hello, I'm Doward (everyone calls me Jake). UX Apprentice at Bloc with 10 years of branding and visual design experience.

Keep It Simple

Formerly Director of Multimedia at Integration Innovation Inc. (i3). I started my career working in product marketing for a large aerospace and communications tech company. I worked at Inergi Design Services a few years afterwards, learning a ton in a short time there.

My background is in photography. I studied at Central St. Martins in London and visual communication for four years at Auburn University, where I won the Lethander Award in the Spring of 2000. I finished my BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Art History in 2006 from the University of Alabama in Huntsville, where I was awarded Best in Show for Print Work in 2006.
